All Games

Here is all of the games on the site. For now

Snake - A classic snake game, but self coded. Eat apples to grow. Use the arrow keys to navigate the canvas.
Tetris - Classic tetris, but simpler and more fun! Move bricks and line them up in rows to score a mass amount of points. Just dont reach the top.
Astray - A game about a ball and a maze. Your objective is to find the exit of the maze using the arrow keys.
DinoGame - The classic offline mode Google Chrome T-Rex run game. It has pretty much everything that the last one has.
Minesweeper - Minesweeper, but simplified. Self coded and works well. Right click to flag and left click to open a tile. Your goal is to flag all of the mines in the area.
Typer - A tower defense like typing game. Characters can be case sensitive or insensitive. Wrong characters decrease the score. Click button to change case sensitivity. Pause by pressing ESC.
Tower Defense - A pretty fun tower defense game made by me in under a week. Feel free to give feedback. Other than that it is pretty addicting.
MicroFPS - The newest version of MicroFPS with a bunch more levels to play and even some more loot to find. There will be more maps and levels added in the future as well.
Old MicroFPS - A pretty fun self-developed game made with Unity. You search througout halls to find robots to destroy. There are many little things to find and more weapons to use.
MicroCart - A self-developed carting game just like Mario Cart. Drive around and zoom through roads.
Microman - Not the best game, but it is pretty fun. It's basically a knockoff Mario but more simple.
